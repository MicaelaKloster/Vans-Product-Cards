Product Info

The Old Skool is Vans' classic shoe and the first model to feature the brand's iconic sidestripe. They were born as a shoe for skaters from the 70's and over the decades it became a basic lifestyle model. They are low-cut shoes made with a resistant upper, they have reinforced toes to add durability, the original Vans Wafflesole rubber sole and padded collar to offer support and comfort.
